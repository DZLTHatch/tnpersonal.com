A shark evolution game where you can choose your own shark, choose your own theme and font size and choose from several games.

There's several games in our game. One is -Shark Clicker-, the other one is -Shark Running- that's made by other team members and I.

Get FUN-NED.

The home page of the website contains a sidebar with 3 different options. You can choose to play a game, change the settings, or choose your shark. Before the project, I didn't know how to let the div container slide on click, but now I do. The sidebar contains several hover effects, including the change of background smoothly and make text glow.
